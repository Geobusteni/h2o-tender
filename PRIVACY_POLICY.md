# Privacy Policy for H2O Tender

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Introduction

H2O Tender ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Weight and Age**: Used to calculate personalized hydration goals
- **Location Data**: Used to determine local climate for accurate hydration recommendations
  - You can provide location manually or allow the app to access your device's location
  - Location data is processed locally and sent to our AI service only when you choose to use automatic location detection
- **Activity Level**: Used to adjust hydration recommendations
- **Sleep/Wake Times**: Used to schedule hydration reminders

### Usage Data
- **Daily Water Consumption**: Tracked locally on your device
- **Reminder Preferences**: Stored locally to customize your experience

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version (for app functionality)
- **App Usage**: Basic analytics to improve app performance (anonymous)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Calculate personalized daily hydration goals
- Schedule smart hydration reminders
- Provide climate-aware hydration recommendations
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Send push notifications for hydration reminders (with your permission)

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- All personal data (weight, age, consumption logs) is stored locally on your device using secure local storage
- Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use features that require cloud services

### Cloud Services
- **Firebase Cloud Functions**: Used only for AI-powered location and climate analysis
  - Location coordinates are sent to our AI service to identify your city and climate
  - No personally identifiable information is stored in Firebase
  - API calls are logged for debugging purposes but do not contain personal data

### Data Security
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
- Local data is encrypted using device-level encryption
- Cloud communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI
- We use OpenAI's API through Firebase Cloud Functions to provide AI-powered features
- When you use location-based features, coordinates are sent to OpenAI to identify your city and climate
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies to data processed by their services: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

### Firebase (Google)
- We use Firebase Cloud Functions for AI proxy services
- Firebase's privacy policy applies: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Control
- You can view and edit your personal information at any time through the Settings screen
- You can delete all app data by using the "Reset All Data" option in Settings
- You can disable location services in your device settings

### Push Notifications
- You can enable or disable push notifications at any time through device settings
- Notification permissions are requested when you first use reminder features

### Location Services
- You can choose to provide location manually instead of using automatic location detection
- Location permissions can be revoked at any time through device settings

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you delete the app or use the "Reset All Data" feature
- **Cloud Data**: No personal data is permanently stored in the cloud
- **Logs**: Firebase function logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging purposes

## Children's Privacy

H2O Tender is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have that information removed.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
